Hi! My name is Nils and I love storytelling through sound.

I am always listening around for inspiring sounds to record and edit. Invoking emotions and creating new worlds in my projects is my passion.

Professionally, I am specialising both in Production Sound and Sound Design for Film. I have started working as a boom operator in 2015 in my home country Germany. In 2017, I then expanded into post production, excited to explore the creative side of film sound. Recently, I have moved to Edinburgh, Scotland to pursue my Sound Design studies at Edinburgh Napier University.

I believe in the bigger picture. In order to construct an excelling movie experience I find it essential to begin the creative sound process as early as possible, when the film writing is still in progress. My experience in screenwriting, which I gained through courses at the University of Edinburgh, have contributed greatly to my overall understanding of filmmaking.

I am always keen to meet new people for a creative collaboration. Feel free to get in touch with me at info@nilsgradlowsky.com
